INTERNATIONAL MEETINGS

A major activity that any businessman takes part in, is a meeting. And one important thing about meetings is that everybody needs to be in the same place for a meeting to occur. But sometimes "everybody" isn't available to attend the meeting, perhaps they're on an international business trip. HoloTrip is the solution to this problem!

Whether you're in a different town or a different continent, setting up a HoloTrip is easy and productive! Once a meeting is set-up and everyone connects, everyone will be able to move around in full 3D and will therefore be able to interact with each other as if they were really at a physical meeting!.

When you can act naturally, everything becomes easier! Collaborate with other colleagues, sending and receiving files are just as easy as passing an object to a friend. You can even open up an application on your HoloLens and display what you're doing via HoloTrip! This is meetings revolutionized.

RAPID COLLABORATION

Collaboration is an important part of making the project deadline. Failing to work together on important aspects of a project can be the difference between a commercial failure and a smash hit product! With HoloTrip, we make collaboration easier, faster and more efficient!

When you open up a program in HoloLens and stream it through HoloTrip, everyone connected to your session will be able to see your progress. But that's not enough. You can also record the streaming session and allow other users to take control of your program.

With HoloTrip, we even have tools to help you keep track of projects. You can create 3D notes and models for easier recollection and then send these notes to your colleagues! Within the first year of launch, HoloTrip will be releasing a GitHub add-on! More information on that later...

PROJECT MANAGEMENT OVERSEAS

When you're working on a big project, it can be stressful. It can be even more stressful when you have to leave home to work on that project overseas. Here at HoloTrip, we can't make all your stress go away - but we can certainly help! Project Management Overseas, only with HoloTrip!

Just like the meetings, you can move around in full 3D to fully express yourself. Combine this with HoloTrip's 3D notes feature and the multitasking ability of HoloLens, your productivity will sky-rocket! You can look at the project from all angles, and even simulate changes to the design.

With the HoloTrip GitHub add-on, you can change the files and notes relating to your project and then push them to your GitHub repository! Keep your data neatly organised with full version control and branching. Physical project, on the web. Only with HoloTrip with GitHub. Coming soon.
