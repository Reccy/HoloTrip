WHAT IS HOLOTRIP?

HoloTrip is a revolutionary program that allows you to virtually project yourself into another person's space, where you can Explore, Work and Play Together.

IMMERSIVE

With the power of HoloLens and Augmented Reality, you'll believe that where HoloTrip takes you is real. Prepare to be amazed.

ACCESSIBLE

Only with HoloTrip and HoloLens will you be able to Explore, Work and Play Together with the power to control your computer naturally.

REALISTIC

Physics calculations are exactly like real life, and when you connect to the NVIDIA GRID™ everything becomes just that bit more amazing.
