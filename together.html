Together.

Experience HoloTrip with friends overseas.

EXPLORE TOGETHER

Everyone loves to discover and explore new things, new places, new cultures, new languages. Everything that is new is exciting, and experiencing your excitement with friends is what HoloTrip is all about! See your friend's home town. Take a tour of Egypt. Anything is possible with HoloTrip!

You can invite friends to your place or join them at theirs! When you connect, you'll see their world form around yours. It's like you're really there! And it's not just you and your friend, up to 25 people can join per session and interact seamlessly, and that's where the real fun is!

If you don't know anybody else with a HoloLens, you can connect to a HoloHub - a virtual 3D chat-room located at real-world landmarks where you can meet new people, play games with them, invite them to your place, join their project, and much more! HoloHub - only with HoloTrip.

WORK TOGETHER

When hands make light work. When you're working, it's better to work together! With HoloTrip we've made it faster and easier to work and collaborate on projects. We've made it so that you don't need to be in the same room as your colleague to be able to see them in full-body 3D!

HoloTrip supports all HoloLens applications and allows you to use HoloLens as normal while in a HoloTrip session. This unlocks a plethora of possibilities and opportunities for your team to collaborate more effectively! A colleague wants the latest documentation? Hand it to them naturally.

You don't even need to be in the same country as your colleagues to work on the same project. Be it engineering, technology or even art sculpting. With HoloTrip we've made it possible for you to inspect your project in full 3D, with up-to-date documentation via GitHub and real-time editing.

PLAY TOGETHER

After all of that exploring and working, we all need to relax and unwind. How about a game of football? Unfortunately the nearest football pitch is 10km away, and you don't want to bother. With HoloTrip, you can play whatever you want, when you want - with friends.

You can play almost anything you can think of! Want to play a simple game of football? Go ahead. Want to save the world from an alien invasion? Go for it! New HoloTrip compatible games are being made all the time that will deliver mind-blowing multiplayer experiences!

Be it a simple AR game or fully-fledged VR gaming - HoloTrip is ready for it! Jet fighters, military operations, mysteries, space, the sea - everything is ready on HoloLens with HoloTrip. This is the future of gaming. Why not experience it with your friends?
